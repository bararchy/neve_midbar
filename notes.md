# General Notes  

### Spencer Tridant  

```
Level 1 - luck, you command it and hope you get what you wish (1d20)
Level 2 - novice, you have some control and can usually get what you want ot happen (1d20 + 2)
Level 3 - Expireanced, you have had enough time to work your link with the weapon to a major degree and can make it do what you wish unless there is some issue (1d20 + 6) 
Level 4 - Master, you have mastered the Tridant and the power of the sea, you can also have one legendery action with Tridant once a long rest (1d20 + 10)
```
